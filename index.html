FAQ
Common questions
What formats does Tara AI support for video upload? +
Tara AI supports all common video formats including MP4, MOV, WebM, AVI, FLV, WMV, MKV and 3GPP. There is no strict file size limit but we recommend keeping uploads under 500MB for the best performance.
What image formats does Image to Prompt support? +
We support JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, HEIC and most other common image formats. You can upload screenshots, photos, concept art, stills from other videos, or any other visual.
Is my uploaded content private and secure? +
Yes. Videos and images you upload are processed in memory and deleted from our servers within 24 hours. We never use your content to train AI models and we never share it with third parties.
Do I need to create an account to use Tara AI? +
No. You can use all tools without creating an account. A free account lets you save your prompt history and access premium features.
Which AI video platform produces the best results? +
It depends on your use case. Google Veo 3 is currently the most powerful for cinematic quality and native audio. Sora is best for realistic scenes. Runway Gen-4 gives the most precise motion control. Tara's prompts describe scenes, camera work and lighting in plain, detailed language that works well across all of them.
How does the Storyboard Generator work? +
Describe your project, number of shots and style. Tara AI writes a complete shot-by-shot storyboard with visual descriptions, camera direction, lighting notes and action cues — ready to paste into any AI video tool or share with your team.
